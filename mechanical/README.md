# Mechanical Design

This folder contains the mechanical and structural design information for the smart glove project.

## Content

- CAD or 3D design files
- assembly notes
- structure explanation
- support design for sensor integration

## Design Goal

The mechanical design was created to:
- hold the sensors in stable positions
- improve integration on the glove
- support future modifications and extensions

## Notes

The glove includes a dedicated structural design for placing the finger sensors and stabilizing the full assembly on the hand.
